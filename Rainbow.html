Rainbow Six Seige


Rainbow Six Siege is a stratigical, tactial shooting game on mulitple platforms.Including
Playstation 4 , Xbox1 , and Pc(Personal Computer).Within in the game you can play different modes
which are Secure the Area, Bomb, and Custom game mode.This game is not a typical story mode where a person would
follow the options and guidelines of the game but rather it is online to where you play with others and do as you want.


Rating

6 out of 10
I give this game a 6 out of 10 for the great graphics and simplicity of enojoyment.The
main downfall would be the constant glitches being found by players.Once
these glitches are exposed and exploited it causes the game to become toxic and annoying.
Beside the glitches I do higly recommend this game.

Price


$18.00-$30.00 ( Depending on where you purchase from)
