Grand Theft auto 5 Online


Grand Theft Auto 5 Online is a mulit-player game where players from all over can connect
via console or pc. In Gta you have the option to roam in freemode , play LTS(Last Team Standing),
Deathmatches, Heist, or regular jobs. A benefit from all of these would be the outcome of being paid, virtually ofcourse


Rating

7 out of 10
I give this game a 7 out of 10 for the great graphics and simplicity of enojoyment.
Although the game is fun to play , it has its moments of annoyance. Such as God Mode glitches, and
the difficulty of getting money.
